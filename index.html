About

The Project

LT
Loris Tatard
Founder — Fundamental Edge
Second-year student · IESEG School of Management
IESEG School of Management — Grande École Programme

Fundamental Edge is a personal equity research platform built by Loris Tatard, a second-year student at IESEG School of Management — combining weekly company analyses, a proprietary financial scoring tool, and an open peer review system where finance professionals can comment on published work.

The goal is simple: build genuine analytical skills before entering the industry, publish the work openly, and develop a real network through the quality of the research. Every analysis follows a rigorous structured methodology. Every verdict is independent. Every piece of professional feedback is published with explicit permission.

The project covers 35+ planned company analyses across technology, luxury, banking, energy, aerospace, pharmaceuticals, logistics and more — with a target of 150+ analyses over four years of study.
